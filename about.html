MEET YOUR DESIGNER

SADIE SELLERS

A Dallas-based designer passionate about creating meaningful connections through thoughtful design and visual storytelling.

Her design journey began unexpectedly during a graphic design internship in New York City, the summer before her senior year at Texas Christian University. Although the role didn't align with her major, the experience sparked a passion for visual storytelling and thoughtful design that quickly became a calling.

Following graduation, she entered the wedding industry and was immediately drawn to the creative possibilities it offered. The Dallas, TX-based designer found purpose in crafting highly personalized details that reflected each client's style and story. The constantly evolving nature of the industry continues to inspire Sadie, and she thrives on exploring fresh ideas, new formats, and unexpected design elements.

What began as designing wedding, birthday, and party invitations for friends and family in her spare time has grown into a full-time business fueled by passion, creativity, and love for meaningful design.
